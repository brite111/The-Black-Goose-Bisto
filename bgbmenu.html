Low and Slow

posted by BGB, November 15, 2016

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food
into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book, Cooking
for Geeks. Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low-tmperature poaching.

Next month, we will be serving Sous Vide Salmon with Dill Hollandaise. To reserve a seat at the chef table, contact us before November 30
